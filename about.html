About Atithi

Get away for the holidays, choose our hotel. What differentiates us from others? A lot of things. Our windows are overlooking the beautiful park. The spacious rooms are decorated with traditional wooden furniture and equipped with free Wi-Fi access, flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms. In our city you can indulge in traditional afternoon tea during the day, catch up with work in the business center and taste delicious food at the best restaurant upon your choice in the evening. We also offer a free airport shuttle service, a 24-hour front desk and a mini-market on site.
